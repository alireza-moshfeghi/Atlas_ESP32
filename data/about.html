About This App

⚙️ Atlas Firmware

Smart Greenhouse Management Middleware

Version: 1.0

Release Date: October 7, 2025

Developer: Alireza Moshfeghi

Contact: moshfeghi79@gmail.com

Atlas Firmware is a lightweight and efficient middleware designed for the ESP32 module. It enables smart greenhouse management by monitoring and controlling environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, CO₂ levels, and soil moisture — helping to automate and optimize plant growth conditions.
